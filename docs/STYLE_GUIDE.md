# Style Guide

## Brief

Thendo Procurement's website should embody sophisticated authority through a dark-mode interface anchored in deep charcoal (#231f20) with strategic gold (#a78147) accents that signal prestige and established expertise, positioning the company as a trusted enterprise partner in mining and industrial compliance. The design language combines minimal, clean layouts with bold typography and refined imagery that celebrates the company's 13+ years of proven track record and Black female ownership, while the sophisticated palette and restrained aesthetic reinforce the high-stakes, regulatory-focused nature of their procurement and compliance services. Every visual element should communicate uncompromising professionalism and South African grounding—this is a partner that delivers transformational results with unwavering integrity in complex industrial landscapes.
